This project analyses conflict events and their human impact using available open datasets. We aim to surface temporal and geographic patterns of violence, displacement and casualties, and to provide interactive visualisations that support exploration and comparison.

Data at hand

  • Event-level conflict data (dates, locations, actors, event types).
  • Casualty and displacement estimates where available.
  • Auxiliary geographic and demographic datasets for context (country/region shapefiles, population).

Preprocessing steps include cleaning event records, normalising location names, aggregating by time windows and regions, and deriving per-capita and trend metrics used in the visualisations.
